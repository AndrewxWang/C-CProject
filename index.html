This website showcases all of the main Creativity and Collaboration projects we worked on throughout this semester.

    • Personally, my favorite project throughout this course was the Identity Music project where we chose to cover a song regardless of our musical capabilities. Overall, I enjoyed majority of the projects and all of the projects pushed me to think outside the box and become more creative.
